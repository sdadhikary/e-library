Help

* Registration: One should be a member of college. He must have college registration id. One can only register if his/her GR_NO should be in database.
    There are four categories for registration
1. Student
2. Staff
3. Librarian
4. Admin
* New user cannot create password of its own. The password will be provided by admin.
* If user forget their password. They can directly contact to admin.
* Student: Student only have the authentication of reading the information provided in the website.
* Staff: Staff have authentication of uploading and reading the information.
* Librarian: They can upload any book or any information.   
* Admin: He have full authentication on the website.
